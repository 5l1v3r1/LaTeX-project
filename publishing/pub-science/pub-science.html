AAAS Science Journals

 

This is the template for Science journal. The template files are downloaded here, but the links for convenience are provided here as well:

  1. The template homepage

However, Science journal is very picky and they do convert your PDFs to HTML and to Word (do not know why they are doing this). And it is probably best to go to the home page and read through everything before doing anything. Still, for those people, who know everything, the template files are provided in this repository.
